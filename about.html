About Michael

Born and raised in Soddy-Daisy. Faith-driven. Here to serve his community, one home at a time.

A Servant's Heart, A Local Story

Michael McBee was born and raised right here in Soddy-Daisy, Tennessee — this isn't just where he works, it's home. He's spent his life around this community, and starting McBee Total Home Solutions was a natural extension of that: a chance to serve the neighbors he grew up around.

Michael is a Christian, and his faith shapes how he shows up to every job. That means honest pricing, honest timelines, and honest work — no shortcuts, and no upselling you on things you don't need. He treats every home he works on like it's his own, because to him, that's just how the job should be done.

Whether it's a small repair or a bigger renovation, Michael brings the same servant's heart to every project: show up, do it right, and leave the home better than he found it.
